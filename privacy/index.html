FanWake — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

FanWake ("the App") is a dual-color flashing alarm clock application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Purchase Data

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information.

Motion Data

If you use the "Shake to Dismiss" alarm feature, the App uses your device's accelerometer to detect shaking. This data is processed locally on your device and is never stored or transmitted.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity
  • We do not collect alarm times, colors, or settings
  • We do not track your usage patterns
  • We do not store any data on external servers
  • All alarm data stays on your device only
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs beyond advertising

Advertising

FanWake displays advertisements to free-tier users via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or contextual ads. This data is processed by Google according to their privacy policy. Premium subscribers do not see any ads.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data from apps that use their services

Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob — Advertising for free-tier users (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — Consent management for advertising

Children's Privacy

FanWake is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
