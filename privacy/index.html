FanWake — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

FanWake ("the App") is a dual-color flashing alarm clock application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Purchase Data

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information.

Motion Data

If you use the "Shake to Dismiss" alarm feature, the App uses your device's accelerometer to detect shaking. This data is processed locally on your device and is never stored or transmitted.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity
  • We do not collect alarm times, colors, or settings
  • We do not track your usage patterns
  • We do not store any data on external servers
  • All alarm data stays on your device only
  • We do not use cookies or track individual users

Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck for privacy-first analytics. TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track individual users. We collect anonymous, aggregated usage data such as screen views, feature usage, and crash reports to improve the app experience. No data is sold to third parties.

Learn more: TelemetryDeck Privacy Policy

Subscriptions

FanWake offers auto-renewable subscriptions managed by Apple:

  • Monthly: $3.99/month
  • Yearly: $19.99/year (includes a 3-day free trial)

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage subscriptions in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • TelemetryDeck — Privacy-first analytics (subject to TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy)

Children's Privacy

FanWake is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
