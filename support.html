FAQ

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your premium subscription will be restored automatically.

The alarm didn't ring — what happened?

Make sure notifications are enabled for FanWake in Settings → Notifications. For best results, keep the app in your recent apps (don't force-quit it).

Can I use my own colors?

Yes! When creating an alarm, tap the rainbow "+" button in the color picker to choose any two custom colors.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FanWake → Cancel Subscription.
